AdSense Black List services

Google AdSense doesn't have too much control on what is sending you. Most of the time is sending low paying adverts. This means that you get very little, usually 0,01 USD for a click.
To better control the adverts you get, google give a functionality called "Competitive Ad Filter". If you are logged in this page is the link to it "filter online".
Our website is a simple container for a public list of low paying websites. You can copy and paste the list to your google account on adsense.
In addition you can post list of websites urls with our "submit" page.
