Google AdSense Black List Chrome Extension

Google AdSense offer you a ie plugin to manage adverts. You can find it on the adsense website.
I didn't like it at all so I thought to build one with for Google Chrome and have even a web site to easily keep a public black list for low paying adverts.
You can find the extension on the Google Chrome Extension Gallery.
